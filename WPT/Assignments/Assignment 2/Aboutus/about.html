Our Achievements

  • 10 Million+ Downloads: Our app has surpassed 10 million downloads, helping us connect with a vast audience across the globe.
  • Expansion to 24 Countries: From its origins in India, Zomato has expanded its services to 24 countries, covering over 10,000 cities worldwide.
  • Hyperpure Success: Hyperpure, our ingredient supply chain solution, has been embraced by over 10,000 restaurants, helping them access high-quality products to enhance their offerings.
  • Delivery of 1.2 Billion Orders: We have facilitated over 1.2 billion food deliveries, bringing convenience and delight to millions of customers.
  • Net Zero Emissions: As part of our commitment to sustainability, Zomato has taken steps to achieve net-zero carbon emissions in the near future.

  • Great Place to Work Certification: Zomato has been certified as a Great Place to Work, acknowledging our commitment to employee satisfaction and growth.
